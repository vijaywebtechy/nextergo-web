What is

Ergonomics

Ergonomics is a science that helps improve products, processes and tools to help people work and live better while helping prevent injury.

The term “ergonomics” is derived from the Greek word ergon meaning work, and nomoi meaning natural laws. It is the science of refining product process and tool design to make them easier or safer for human use. Ergonomics is frequently referred to as human factors engineering.
